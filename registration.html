ICA6NT 2026 Registration Guidelines

At least one of the authors of each accepted paper must register for the conference for the paper to be included in the conference proceedings.

All accepted and presented papers will be forwarded for possible inclusion in the IEEE Xplore digital library (Under approval).

Full registration includes the registration of one paper. Additional papers for a single registration come with an additional fee. The maximum length of the paper is 6 pages including figures, tables, and references.

Registration fee covers admission to all sessions, cost of publishing the article in conference proceedings, welcome reception, conference kit, refreshments, working lunch, banquet dinner and half-a-day tour to nearby places.

A fee of Rs. 500 will be applied for each additional page (with a maximum of 2 pages).
